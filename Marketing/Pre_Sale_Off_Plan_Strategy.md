# Pre-Sale & Off-Plan Strategy / პრისეილის (Off-Plan) სტრატეგია

Since construction starts in spring, we are selling a **"Vision & Financial Opportunity"** rather than physical walls. For high-tier agencies and GCC buyers, the biggest barrier is **Trust (Completion Risk)**.

რადგან მშენებლობა გაზაფხულზე იწყება, ჩვენ ვყიდით "ხედვას და ფინანსურ შესაძლებლობას". მთავარი ბარიერი აქ არის ნდობა - დაასრულებს თუ არა დეველოპერი პროექტს.

---

## 1. The "Trust Bridge" (How to close the gap?) / ნდობის მოპოვების გზები

### A. Financial Security (Escrow & Bank Guarantees)
*   **The Hook**: We remove the "Completion Risk" entirely.
*   **Technical Model**: We use **Escrow accounts** and **Bank Guarantees** for all off-plan transactions. 
*   **Benefit**: The buyer's funds are not released to the developer until specific construction milestones (e.g., foundation, structure finish) are met and verified by an independent third party.
*   **Message**: *"Your investment is protected by a technical security model. We only access your funds when the work is physically done and verified. This is the highest level of financial safety for off-plan property internationally."*

### C. The "Eagle Hills" Proximity
*   **The Hook**: High-tier agencies follow established whales.
*   **Message**: *"Mohamed Alabbar (Eagle Hills) doesn't invest in empty fields without a plan. We are part of the same strategic development zone. Buying now is like buying in Dubai Hills 10 years ago."*
