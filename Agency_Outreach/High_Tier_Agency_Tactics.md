# High-Tier Agency Outreach: The "Hook" Strategy / სოლიდური სააგენტოების მოზიდვის სტრატეგია

To attract "Solid" agencies (Knight Frank, Savills, Sotheby's, Metropolitan), we must move beyond just "offering a commission." These agencies care about their reputation and providing something unique to their HNW (High Net Worth) clients.

სოლიდური სააგენტოების (მაგ. Knight Frank, Savills) მოსაზიდად მხოლოდ მაღალი პროცენტი არ კმარა. მათთვის პრიორიტეტია საკუთარი რეპუტაცია და კლიენტებისთვის ექსკლუზიური შეთავაზების მიწოდება.

## 1. The "Security Hook" / ფინანსური გარანტია
Large agencies fear "scams" or unfinished projects.
*   **The Bait**: Focus on the **Technical Escrow Model** and the **Eagle Hills (Mohamed Alabbar)** presence.
*   **Action**: Lead with: *"We are part of the new $3.5Bn investment corridor in Georgia (neighboring Eagle Hills). We operate a full Escrow and Bank Guarantee model, meaning your clients' funds are only released upon verified construction milestones. This is the highest safety standard in the region."*

## 2. The "Missing Asset" Strategy / "ვაკუუმის" შევსების სტრატეგია
Top-tier agencies in Dubai/Riyadh are overloaded with high-rise apartments. They LACK a "Privacy-First Cool Mountain" product.
*   **The Bait**: Position Green Canyon as the *only* solution for clients who hate the heat but want privacy.
*   **Action**: Pitch it as: *"The missing piece in your portfolio. Your clients have luxury apartments; they need a private mountain sanctuary 2.5 hours away."*

## 3. The "Golden Block" (Exclusivity) / ექსკლუზიური ბლოკი
Solid agencies want to feel special. 
*   **The Bait**: Offer them **Exclusivity** for a specific block (e.g., "Zone B").
*   **Action**: *"We are reserving 5 units in the 'Royal Sector' exclusively for [Agency Name] clients for 30 days. These units provide the highest level of visual privacy on the market."*

## 4. White-Label Marketing / "თეთრი ეტიკეტი"
These agencies have strict branding rules.
*   **The Bait**: provide high-quality "White-Label" (no developer brand) videos and 3D tours that THEY can brand as their own.
*   **Action**: Give them the Gamma presentation link so they can clone it and add their logo in 60 seconds.

## 5. The "Fly-and-Buy" Support / "ჩამოფრინდი და იყიდე"
*   **The Bait**: A guaranteed high-end VIP experience for their clients in Georgia.
*   **Action**: *"You provide the client, we provide the VIP transport, a private chef dinner on the canyon edge, and a helicopter site visit (if budget allows per client level)."*
