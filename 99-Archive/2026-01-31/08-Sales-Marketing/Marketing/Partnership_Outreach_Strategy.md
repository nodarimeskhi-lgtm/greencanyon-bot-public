# Partnership Outreach Strategy: Eagle Hills & Agencies / პარტნიორობის სტრატეგია

## 1. The "Ecosystem" Pitch (For Eagle Hills Directly)
**Strategy**: Do not compete. **Complement**.
Eagle Hills sells "Tbilisi Waterfront" (City) and "Gonio Marina" (Sea). They are missing only one thing: **The Mountain**.

**The Pitch**:
*"You own the City and the Sea. Let us be your exclusive Mountain partner. When your client buys a $500k apartment in Tbilisi, offer them a 'Summer Retreat' in our Green Canyon at a special partner rate. We complete your ecosystem."*

**Actionable Steps / ნაბიჯები:**
1.  **Visit the HQ**: Go to their Sales Office on **Rustaveli Avenue**. Ask for the "Sales Director" (not a junior agent).
2.  **The Offer**: Propose a "Referral Commission" (e.g., 5-7%) if they send their HNW clients to you.

## 3. How to Interest Them? (The "Hook")
Agencies and Developers only care about **Volume** and **Ease of Sale**.

*   **Hook 1: "The Missing Asset":** "Your Saudi clients are asking for cool weather. You can't sell them Tbilisi in July (35°C). You *need* our inventory to keep them happy."
*   **Hook 2: "High Commission / Fast Pay":** "We offer higher commission than standard market rates for bulk deals."
