# GCC & Saudi Arabia Investor Attraction Strategy: "Green Canyon / EDGE VILLAGE"

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Cultural Adaptation is Key**: The strategy relies on positioning the project not just as "Real Estate" but as a "Private Halal-Friendly Family Retreat" in a cool climate.

## 1. Product Adaptation for GCC Market

### Privacy & Family Focus (Critical)
*   **"The Family Compound" Concept**: Market the *Comfort+* and *Grand Villa* units not as individual houses but as private family estates.
*   **Modifications**:
    *   Ensure high perimeter fences or natural green barriers for private pools invillas (visual privacy is non-negotiable for many Saudi families).
    *   Separate "Men's Majlis" and "Family Living" areas in larger villa floor plans if customization is possible.
*   **Amenities**:
    *   Designate specific "Women Only" hours for the common Infinity Pool or Wellness Center.
    *   Highlight "Starlink" connectivity as "Work from Mountains" for the family head while the family vacations.

### The "Cool Summer" Proposition
*   **Primary Pain Point**: excessively hot summers in Riyadh/Jeddah (45°C+).
*   **Solution**: "Own your Summer Escape at 20°C". Marketing materials should prominently display the temperature difference.
