# Marketing Strategy: Project Tsalka

Tsalka is a unique high-altitude region in Georgia, which has become a new tourist magnet thanks to the Dashbashi Canyon and its cool climate. This strategy proposes three paths for property promotion.

---

## Variant 1: "Investment Springboard" (Focus on Yield)

**Target Audience:** Local and international investors looking for projects in the early growth stage.

**Concept:** Tsalka is the "new Bakuriani," but with a unique natural profile and strong state support for tourism.

**Key Message:** "Your capital at the center of a new tourist wave. Higher yields than in Tbilisi."

**Advantages:**
*   Land and property value growth (+20-30% per year).
*   Built-in tourist flow thanks to the Dashbashi Glass Bridge.
*   Rental management options through a management company.

**Channels:**
*   YouTube reviews: "Where to invest in Georgia."
*   Webinars for overseas investors.
*   Facebook/Instagram ads focusing on data and ROI.

---

## Variant 2: "The Alpine Escape" (Focus on Leisure and Nature)

**Target Audience:** Tbilisi residents (families, remote workers) tired of summer heat and smog.

**Concept:** A private residence in a place where summer is always cool and the air is purest.

**Key Message:** "Escape the summer heat. 1,500 meters above comfort level."

**Advantages:**
*   Temperatures 10-12 degrees lower than in Tbilisi.
*   Proximity to the lake and national parks.
*   A 2-hour drive from the capital — the perfect "weekend cottage" format.

**Channels:**
*   Targeting Tbilisi residents during peak heat (June-August).
*   Collaborations with travel influencers.
*   Weekend ads ("Come for a viewing and a picnic").

---

## Variant 3: "Uncompromising Galt" (Premium Quality)

**Target Audience:** People who value status and flawless execution.

**Concept:** Wild nature outside the window, technological comfort inside. A combination of rugged architecture and panoramic glazing by Galt.

**Key Message:** "Aesthetics in wild conditions. Quality that is not afraid of heights."

**Advantages:**
*   Use of top-tier glazing systems for energy efficiency.
*   Modern architectural design integrated into the landscape.
*   Galt service — from foundation to the last bolt.

**Channels:**
*   Private presentations for Galt's regular clients.
*   Publications in architectural magazines (Architectural Digest Georgia, etc.).
*   Direct marketing (VIP newsletters).
