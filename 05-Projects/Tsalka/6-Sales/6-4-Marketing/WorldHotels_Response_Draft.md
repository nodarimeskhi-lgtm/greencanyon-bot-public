**Subject:** Green Canyon Eco Village & Resort – Affiliation Proposal & Counter-Proposal

Dear Stefan,

Thank you for providing the affiliation proposal for the Green Canyon Eco Village & Resort under the WorldHotels Crafted collection. We highly value the reputation of BWH Hotels and see great potential in aligning our unique Eco-Village concept with your global distribution network.

Our team has thoroughly reviewed the commercial terms. While we are comfortable with the majority of the operational structure, there are a few critical points that heavily impact the financial modeling of our real estate and development strategy. We would like to address these below to find a mutually beneficial middle ground:

**1. Real Estate Units Sales Fee (2.50% of Gross Real Estate Sales)**
This is our primary concern. Our development model relies on selling "Lifestyle Eco Residences" and cottages to individual investors, primarily from the GCC region. The primary value drivers for our buyers are our unique eco-friendly concept, the halal-compliant privacy features, and the exceptional natural location of Tsalka—rather than the WorldHotels brand name acting as the primary premium driver (as is typically the case with "Hard Brands" like Four Seasons or Ritz-Carlton). 
Applying a 2.5% fee on the gross asset value significantly disrupts our project’s financial viability and investor margins. We kindly request to either waive this fee entirely or replace it with a nominal, flat administrative integration fee per unit sold.

**2. Notice Period (24 Months)**
A 24-month notice period is unusually long and restricts our operational flexibility. We request to adjust the termination notice period to a standard 12 months.

**3. Inventory & Phased Opening**
The proposal currently states 60 rooms. Please note that our master plan includes a larger inventory (approximately 100 apart-hotel keys and several dozen premium cottages). However, we plan to onboard this inventory in phases. We would appreciate confirmation that the agreement accommodates a phased integration of units without incurring penalties.

**4. Loyalty Program Cumulative Costs**
While we understand the value of the WorldHotels Rewards program, the combination of the 5.5% Participation Fee on top of the standard GRR fee represents a substantial cost for stays generated through this channel. We would like to discuss potential caps on the required free night inventory during our high-season periods.

**5. Global Exchange Program for Apartment Owners**
As our model heavily relies on attracting high-net-worth individuals to purchase the units, providing them with global lifestyle benefits is crucial. We would like to explore the possibility of integrating our apartment owners into a reciprocal "Owner Exchange Program" or granting them a high-tier status in the WorldHotels Rewards program. Specifically, we want to offer them the perk of exchanging a portion of their allocated free nights at Green Canyon for equivalent free nights at other WorldHotels properties globally. This would be a massive selling point for the real estate phase.

Green Canyon is set to be a landmark premium destination in Georgia, and we believe WorldHotels Crafted is a fitting partner for our vision. However, for this partnership to make financial sense for our investors, we need to restructure the Real Estate Sales Fee component and secure valuable perks for our buyers.

We would be happy to schedule a call next week to discuss these points further and finalize the framework.
