# MANTRA RWA Incubator - Request for Information (RFI)

**Project Name:** Green Canyon Eco Village
**Asset Class:** Hospitality / Premium Eco-Resort Real Estate
**Location:** Tsalka, Georgia (Dashbashi Canyon edge)
**Applicant:** Galt / Green Canyon (Developer)

## 1. Project Overview
Green Canyon Eco Village is a 38-hectare premium resort development located on the edge of the spectacular Dashbashi Canyon in Georgia. The project encompasses a 1000-room hotel complex, luxury villas, and extensive recreational infrastructure. We are seeking to conduct a Pilot Security Token Offering (STO) for our high-yield hospitality units to access global Web3 liquidity, specifically targeting the MENA and global investor base via the MANTRA network.

## 2. The Pilot STO Asset (Phase 1)
To prove the model and build trust, our initial STO will focus on a curated pool of high-yield assets.
*   **Asset:** 20 Premium A-Frame Eco-Cottages (Fully finished, furnished, and land-backed).
*   **Total Asset Valuation (Fund Size):** $3,000,000 USD
*   **Target Raise via Tokenization:** 80% ($2,400,000 USD)
*   **Developer Retained Equity:** 20% (Demonstrating long-term "Skin in the game").
*   **Construction Status:** Under development / Off-plan.

## 3. Proposed Tokenomics & Yield Structure
*   **Token Name:** Green Canyon Token (GCT)
*   **Token Price:** $50.00 USD per token
*   **Total Supply per STO:** 60,000 Tokens
*   **Projected Rental Yield (Net ROI):** ~10% APY. The net operating revenue from the resort operations will be distributed to token holders (e.g., in stablecoins) via smart contract payouts.
*   **Capital Appreciation Target:** +20% upon construction completion.
*   **Target IRR for Investors:** 15% - 18%.
*   **MANTRA Community Incentive:** We are open to embedding $OM staking utilities. For instance, offering a 5% discount on the token presale price exclusively for $OM stakers, or providing ecosystem utility perks (e.g., fractional free stays at the resort) for top $OM / GCT holders.

## 4. Legal & Compliance Strategy
We currently operate under a Georgian LLC framework, managing the physical real estate according to Georgian construction and property laws. We recognize that accessing MANTRA’s institutional and retail liquidity may require establishing a globally recognized Web3 SPV (e.g., within the UAE / ADGM / DIFC). We are fully open to restructuring the SPV ownership according to VARA and MANTRA compliance requirements to ensure a seamless primary offering.

## 5. Objectives from MANTRA
We approach MANTRA to act as the core issuance, distribution, and incubator platform for this pilot STO. We are seeking:
1.  **Technical & Legal Engine:** ERC-3643 compliant smart contract deployment, identity management (KYC/AML), and regulatory advisory for onboarding real Georgian assets.
2.  **Market Distribution:** Direct access to MANTRA’s engaged RWA investor community.
3.  **Secondary Market Integration:** Listing the tokenized assets to provide liquidity for our investors.

*We look forward to discussing the feasibility of this pilot STO and establishing a long-term partnership for the phased tokenization of the remaining $150M+ inventory of the Green Canyon development.*
