# Sales Office Execution Plan / გაყიდვების ოფისის სამოქმედო გეგმა

## 1. The Strategy: "Sniper vs. Shotgun" / სტრატეგია
We do not spam. We target specific high-value partners.
ჩვენ არ ვსპამავთ. ჩვენ ვესაუბრებით კონკრეტულ მაღალი ღირებულების პარტნიორებს.

## 2. Daily Routine for Sales Agent / გაყიდვების მენეჯერის დღის განრიგი

| Time | Activity | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **10:00 - 11:00** | **Research & List Building** | Find 10 new agents/brokers on LinkedIn from target countries (e.g., "Real Estate Broker Riyadh"). |
| **11:00 - 13:00** | **Outreach (Cold DM/Email)** | Send 20 individualized messages using the templates. Attach the *correct* PDF (Family vs. Yield). |
| **13:00 - 14:00** | *Lunch Break* | |
| **14:00 - 16:00** | **Follow-up Calls** | Call leads who opened the email but didn't reply. script below. |
| **16:00 - 17:00** | **CRM Update** | Log everything. If it's not in CRM, it didn't happen. |

## 3. Scripts (What to say?) / სკრიპტები

### A. LinkedIn Connection Note (Generic)
*"Hi [Name], I noticed you work with HNW clients in Riyadh. We are launching a private, halal-friendly eco-resort in Georgia (2 hours away). I think your clients looking for 'summer escape' would love it. Open to a 5-min chat?"*

### B. Cold Call Opening (The "Hook")
*"Hello [Name], this is [Your Name] from Edge Village Georgia. I'm calling because I see you sell luxury property in Dubai.
I know your clients are tired of the summer heat. We have a project 2 hours away that is 20 degrees cooler and offers 12% yield.
Can I send you a 1-page summary?"*

## 4. KPIs (Targets) / საკონტროლო მაჩვენებლები

*   **Daily**:
    *   20 New Outreach Messages.
    *   10 Follow-up Calls.
*   **Weekly**:
    *   5 Scheduled Zoom Meetings / Factory Visits.
    *   1 Signed Agency Agreement.

## 5. Tools Setup / ტექნიკური მხარე
1.  **WhatsApp Business**: Configure "Quick Replies" with links to the 3 Presentations.
2.  **Gamma Account**: Ensure every agent knows how to generate a custom link for a client.
3.  **CRM**: Create pipeline stages: "New Lead" -> "Presentation Sent" -> "Meeting Booked" -> "Agency Signed".

## 6. How to Handle Objections / პასუხები პრეტენზიებზე

**Objection**: "Georgia is risky."
**Answer**: *"Actually, we just signed an MOU with the Government (send Memo). Plus, Eagle Hills (Mohamed Alabbar) just put $3B here. It's the safest growing market right now."*

**Objection**: "My clients only buy ready units."
**Answer**: *"Understood. But by the time it's ready, the price will be 40% higher. This is for your 'smart investors' who want capital appreciation, not just rental income."*
