# Agency Outreach: Communication Channels & Tactics / სააგენტოებთან კომუნიკაციის არხები

To reach "Solid" agencies abroad, especially in the GCC (Saudi, UAE, Kuwait), you need a multi-channel approach. In these markets, **WhatsApp** is as professional as Email, and **LinkedIn** is the gatekeeper.

სოლიდურ სააგენტოებთან კავშირის დასამყარებლად საჭიროა მულტი-არხობრივი მიდგომა. GCC ბაზარზე (საუდის არაბეთი, საამიროები) **WhatsApp** ისეთივე პროფესიული არხია, როგორც მეილი, ხოლო **LinkedIn** საუკეთესოა პირველადი კონტაქტისთვის.

---

## 1. Primary Channels (The "Where?") / მთავარი არხები

### A. LinkedIn (Direct Outreach to Decision Makers)
*   **Target**: Look for "Head of International Sales", "Partner Relations Manager", or "CEO" of real estate agencies.
*   **Tactic**: Do not send a sales pitch immediately. Send a "Connection Request" with a short 2-sentence note.
*   **Keywords**: "Exclusive Inventory", "Escrow Safety", "GCC Privacy Standard".

### B. WhatsApp Business (The "Closer")
*   **Context**: In Dubai/Riyadh, business moves on WhatsApp.
*   **Tactic**: Once you have a connection on LinkedIn or Email, move to WhatsApp. Send a high-quality "Teaser Video" (15-30 sec) of the canyon/villa.
*   **Rule**: Keep it visual. No long texts. Use voice notes for a personal touch (only if your English/Arabic is good).

### C. Specialized Broker Groups (The "Underground" Channel)
*   **Channel**: Telegram and WhatsApp groups for real estate brokers in Dubai/Istanbul.
*   **Tactic**: Join groups like "Dubai Real Estate Network". Post your "Unique Angle": *"High-Privacy Mountain Villas in Georgia (Cool Summer). 12% Yield. Escrow Protected. 6% Commission for Brokers."*

### D. Targeted Email (The "Institutional" Channel)
*   **Tactic**: Use this ONLY for sending the full Investment PDF after the first chat. 
*   **Subject Line**: "Investment Opportunity: Escrow-Protected Eco-Resort (Green Canyon Georgia)"

---

## 2. The "7-Touch" Sequence / კომუნიკაციის 7-ნაბიჯიანი თანმიმდევრობა
Agencies are busy. They need multiple "touches" to notice you.

1.  **Day 1**: LinkedIn Connection with a personalized note.
2.  **Day 2**: Personalized Email with the "Pre-Launch Teaser".
3.  **Day 4**: LinkedIn Follow-up (Ask: *"Did you get a chance to see the visual on Slide 3?"*).
4.  **Day 6**: WhatsApp message (if number found): *"Hi [Name], I'm [Your Name] from Georgia. Just sent you an invite for a brief Zoom."*
5.  **Day 8**: Zoom Briefing (15 mins): Present the "Pre-Sale Strategy".
6.  **Day 15**: Send a "Construction Update" video (even if it's just a fence being built).
7.  **Month 1**: Invitation to a "Fam-Trip" or "Sunset Picnic" on-site.

---

## 3. Local "Gatekeeper" Strategy / ადგილობრივი პარტნიორები
Don't always try to go direct. Use "Referral" channels:
*   **Georgian Embassies/Trade Rooms**: Ask for lists of accredited agencies in Riyadh/Dubai.
*   **Tourism Agencies**: High-end travel planners in GCC often know families looking to buy "Summer Homes". Offer them a referral fee.
