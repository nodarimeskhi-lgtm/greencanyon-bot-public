**Text:**

## Slide 6: Sustainability (EBRD/GEF Compliance)

**Text:**

*   **Energy Efficiency:** Use of IE3/IE4 High-Efficiency Motors and VFD drives in the factory.
*   **Certifications:** Commitment to **ISO 9001** (Quality), **ISO 14001** (Environment), and **HACCP** (Food Safety).

**Visual Prompt:** Diagram demonstrating the cycle: Waste -> Biogas -> Energy -> Greenhouse -> Food -> Waste.
