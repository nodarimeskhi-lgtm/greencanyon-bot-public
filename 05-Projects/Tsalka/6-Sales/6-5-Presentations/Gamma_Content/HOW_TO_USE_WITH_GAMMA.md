# How to Generate Presentations with Gamma AI

I have prepared 4 detailed content files for you. Follow these steps to turn them into beautiful presentations using Gamma.

## Step 1: Locate Your Content
Go to the `Gamma_Content` folder. You will find:
1.  `Gamma_Content_Family_Sanctuary.md` (For Arab Market)
2.  `Gamma_Content_High_Yield.md` (For Investors)
3.  `Gamma_Content_Eco_Nomad.md` (For Europe/Digital Nomads)
4.  `Gamma_Content_CIS_Regional.md` (For CIS/Locals)

## Step 2: Open Gamma
1.  Go to [gamma.app](https://gamma.app).
2.  Click **"Create new"**.
3.  Select **"Paste text"** (or "Text to Deck").

## Step 3: Copy & Paste
1.  Open one of the `.md` files I created (e.g., `Gamma_Content_Family_Sanctuary.md`).
2.  **Copy** all the text inside.
3.  **Paste** it into Gamma's text box.
4.  Gamma will ask you to confirm the "Outline" (Slides). It should automatically detect the slide breaks (Slide 1, Slide 2, etc.).
5.  Click **Continue**.

## Step 4: Choose a Theme
*   **Family Sanctuary:** Choose a theme that is "Elegant", "Soft", or "Nature-inspired" (Greens, Beiges).
*   **High Yield:** Choose a "Professional", "Corporate", or "Dark Mode" theme (Blues, Darks, Gold).
*   **Eco-Nomad:** Choose a "Fresh", "Playful", or "Minimalist" theme.
*   **CIS Regional:** Choose a "Cozy" or "Warm" theme.

## Step 5: Refine
Gamma will generate the deck.
*   **Images:** Gamma selects AI images. If you don't like one, click on the image -> "Web Image Search" -> Type keywords from my **Visual Prompt** (e.g., "Misty green canyon").
*   **Charts:** You can add real charts in Gamma manually if needed, using the data provided in the text.

## Step 6: Export
*   Click **Share** -> **Export**.
*   Choose **PowerPoint** or **PDF**.
